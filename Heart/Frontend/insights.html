Predictor attributes and their values

by Pimpa Sophia Akuffo on May. 19th 2019

Name of Predictor Data Type Meaning and Value
Age Continous Age of the patient in years e.g 54
Sex Discrete Gender of patient. 1=Female, 0=Male
Cp Discrete Type of chest pain exhibited by patient. 0= Typical Angina, 1= Atypical Angina, 2= Non-Anginal Pain, 3= Asymptomatic Pain
Trestbps Continous The resting blood pressure of the patient measuerd in "mm Hg"
Chol Continous The patient's cholesterol measured in mg/dl
F.B.S Discrete The patient's blood sugar level. If > 120 mg/dl, 1= True, 0= False
Restecg Discrete Resting electrocardiographic measurement. 0= Normal, 1= ST-T wave abnormality, 2= Left ventricular hypertrophy
Thalach Continous The patient's maximum heart rate achieved
Exang Discrete Exercise induced angina: 1= Yes, 0= No
Oldpeak Continous ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
Slope Discrete The slope of peak exercise. 0= upsloping, 1= flat, 2= downsloping
Ca Discrete Number of major vessels
Thal Discrete Thalassemia, a blood disease. 0= normal, 1= fixed detect, 2= reversable defect
